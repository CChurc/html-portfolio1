The Best Movies of All Time According to Christina

My top 10 Movies

An annual favorite for the holidays. But it is more then that. Its a masterclass on living your authentic self, whether you know it or not.

Jaws

The fear it gives you isnt all production, its the unknown monster. Quotable quotes, deep characters and moving plot. A must see before visiting the ocean
